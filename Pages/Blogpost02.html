by me

Ok so you've decided to learn web development. And you have to build a site after site (fun!) but you also need to pick out colours, find images, find text (no so fun!) to make your website feel more real. So here are a few of my favourites, straight from my source library.

lorem ipsum

We keep seeing paragraphs of latin (like old school, spell casting latin) bulking out our sample websites. What is it? Why do people keep using it? Lorem ipsum is a well used placeholder text. It's a simple copy and paste into your design to test how a few paragraphs of text will look. Use it as a placeholder for your text so you can adjust your design around a full page.

Check out the Lorem Ipsum generator here: Lorem Ipsum

placeholder images

So now that we have our placeholder text, what about placeholder images? Yes, there is a website for that and it is literally called placeholder.com.

Check out the Placeholder here: Placeholder

coolors

Putting together websites when you are learning how to get them working is one thing. Making them look nice is a whole nother thing. At least with this colour scheme generator, it's one less thing to think of.

Check out Coolors here: Coolors
